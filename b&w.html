Magsaysay inaugurates new conference room

Published: Friday, 04 December 2015

The new conference room of Ramon Magsaysay High School (RMHS) was opened for students and teachers use last October 13, 2015 after almost six months of construction.

The School administration initiated a renovation plan when some teachers suggested for bigger conference room, according to RMHS accountant, Ms . Susan Rivera.

They thought of an idea to find a more spacious area to house the conference room, and decided to put it in the records section of the school to give way for its construction.

Functions of the conference room will be for: Department head meetings, master teachers’ monthly meetings, and other events, one of which is the closing ceremony of the Boys and Girls week for the division.

Funds for the renovation were shared by the School’s Maintenance Other Operating Expense (MOOE), Youth Entrepreneurship Cooperation School (YECS), RMHS Multi-purpose Cooperative (RMHSFMPC), RMHS Retail Laboratory store and the school office savings.

Ms .Rivera added that the records section was transferred to a more convenient area which is at the first floor of the Special Science Building (SSB) near the entrance where request for student forms and permanent records is accessible.

Recently, the new conference room held MAPEH and Science departments’ film showing activities, and English Quiz Bee.

Meanwhile, the computer room was moved to the second floor of the main building and the old conference room will be renovated to give way for a new Audio-Visual Room (AVR).

The Audio-Visual Room once fully equipped and renovated however, will be used for other assemblies, events and local competitions.

(Angel Arnecilla)
